Mystery of Anya and Anubhav : Part 1

Chapter 3 : Love Or Secret

After feeling defeated and lost, Anubhav woke up with a headache, a reminder of the rainy night before. He didn't want to fall ill, not with only few days he had to make a change. He decided to head to the nearest medical store to get some medicine and prevent any sickness from taking hold. As he was about to leave, something caught his eye. He rummaged through the evidence he had collected and found a piece of paper labeled "Khurana Medical Shop, Street-04, Noida." This was the same store that Anya used to visit every month for her medication. Anubhav packed his bag, locked his room and set off towards the store with a glimmer of hope.

On the bus ride there, Anubhav couldn't help but think about Anya. He pulled out his phone and reread her diary entries from July 25, 2015. "It's the season of falling in love with the beauty of raindrops. I want to live in the moment, not waste it on work. That's why I took the day off. But, like all holidays, it reminds you of all the work you've left unfinished. I have to go with Neha to buy some essentials and groceries, it's the last week of the month. But I didn't want to miss out on this beautiful weather. So I decided to go out in the rain without an umbrella, madness I know, but I didn't want anything to come between me and the raindrops." Anubhav smiled at the memory.


He continued reading, "I'm back with a lot of things I bought, but something happened today. Oh, what a lovely moment it was. Shh.h.sh... Dear Emily, I have a secret to share, don't tell anyone. While I was enjoying the heavy rain, I slipped. I was about to fall but someone caught me, a handsome, good-looking man. I don't know his name, but it felt like a scene straight out of a 90s romantic movie. My heart was beating so loudly that I could hear it. For a moment, I thought I had found someone to hold my hand for the rest of my life, but he let go and walked away. I wish I could see him again." Anubhav realized that the man was him, he couldn't help but feel a pang of longing for Anya.

As the bus pulled up to Street-04, Anubhav wiped away the tears that threatened to fall. He took a deep breath, put his phone away, picked up his bag and stepped off the bus, the medical store in sight. The hope of finding answers at the store and at Anya's office kept him going. Will his journey lead to answers or more twists and turns? Follow Anubhav on his quest for the truth in the next installment.
